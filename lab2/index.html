Lab 1: Beginner training for sending emails in SalesForce

How to edit and test an email in Salesforce Marketing Cloud.

By Renee Krueger


Step 1: Log in to SalesForce Marketing Cloud (SFMC), upon login the dashboard will open.

To begin, please access this link below to log in to SFMC. Go to SalesForce to log in

From the SFMC Dashboard, look for the top menu option to open Email Studio.


Step 2: Next, open the SFMC Email Studio.

Select Email Studio from the menu, drop down to Email, and select. This will take you to the Email Builder. From the Email Builder, look at the top menu for the option open Content. Select Content.


Step 3: From Email Studio, open Content Builder.

On the left half of the screen, you will see all of the content your organization has created. Within your organization’s Content Builder folder, you will see separate folders for images, messages and templates. The documents and logos are subfolders within the images folder. On the right half of the screen you will see all the emails and images uploaded. Click on the email you would like to edit.


Step 4: Edit an existing SFMC email for practice.

An information box opens to display information about email you selected to edit. Next, look at the top right of your screen for a blue button that says Edit. Select Edit, dropping down to Edit Content.


Step 5: Editing the SFMC email content.
  • Change the title
  • Update the body text
  • Provide new images
  • Replace the links

These are examples of changes that you can make easily. Notice the CSS is programmed in styles links, so the look remains the same. Best practices recommend bolding and underlining links for accessibility. Several sections, like the lower links section and footer stay the same and do not need editing.


Step 6: Send a test email to yourself.

warning Select your email as the recipient. Next, change the sender to the appropriate selection for the online publication. In the subject line, note FOR APPROVAL or TEST. Then, when you are ready to forward the test to the group of reviewers, they will clearly understand that this is a test in need of review before distribution. *Once approved, the next section will illustrate how to send the approved email to an attached list.

*Instructions for sending to an attached list in the next training.
